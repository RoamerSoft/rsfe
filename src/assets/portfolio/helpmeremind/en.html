Help Me Remind

Keeping remembering things easy and simple.

Help Me Remind is designed to make remembering things easy and simple. Just fill in a title and a note and choose whether to show the reminder directly or on a future date and time. The reminders will be shown as notifications and will exist until the user swipes them away, even after a phone reboot! The clean and simple design makes this app useable for everyone. In addition the reminders will not drain your battery because of the minimalistic design and efficient programming.

Technics: Native Android Java with Google's material design.
