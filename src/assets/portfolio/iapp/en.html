The Interaction App

The communication bridge between a presenter and the audience.

For a company in Amsterdam I developed a cross platform mobile application (IOS, Android and the web) which facilitates the communication between a presenter and a big audience. The presenter is able to ask questions with the app by using for example a multiple choice question to which the audience can respond. All the answers are received on the presenters second screen and he can use this new information in his presentation. Apart from that, the audience can ask questions to the presenter by using the app as well.

Technics: Angular, Ionc, Cordova, Socket.io, Express.js
Website: play.google.com
Date: 2019-06
Client: WT interactive
